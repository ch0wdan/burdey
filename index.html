FAQS

How do I get to the resort?

The resort sits on a beautiful beach in the northwest of Costa Rica located in Playa Matapalo. The easiest method is to fly into LIR (Liberia) airport in Costa Rica. From there it is only 33km away, which equates to about a 45 minute drive to the resort. You can email (customerservice.guanacaste@riu.com or reception.guanacaste@riu.com) or call the resort to arrange a direct transfer from the airport to the hotel. Alternatively you can also hire a taxi when at the airport.

Can I stay at a different place but still go to the wedding?

Absolutely, however please please talk to us as soon as possible if you are wanting to do this. At least 80% of our guests have to stay at the resort, so we need to keep track of this. There is a day fee to come to the resort for the day.

I want to go but it is too expensive.

First, please feel NO pressure whatsoever to come. If you got an invite, we love you and would love to celebrate with you, but we understand the financial burden it can cause. We still love you and dont measure your love by if you were able to be there physically or not :) Second, here are some creative solutions to save some extra $$$s -The more people in one room the less it will cost over all. For example, for one person in their own room it is around $250/night. Where as with two people it is about $175/person/night. It continues to go down slightly with more people, but not a significant amount -Here are some helpful hacks I have found to get cheaper airfare: Number one is flexibility. Find the flights that you want before making your reservations for accomodation. Being able to give or take a couple of days for your departure and arrival can help a lot. Make sure you are always searching for flights in a private browser (incognito on Chrome). My favorite search engine to play around with is google flights. Get creative with mix and matching one way tickets, this is often how I find stellar deals. When you have routes you want, watch the prices for a little bit -- see how they change through the week -- you will then get a sense of what a good deal is for the flight. Flight tracker email reminders can be super helpful if you forget to check daily.

If you know Jordan and I, you know we are all about just being yourself & comfortable. So whatever makes you happy, makes us happy :) As for the wedding, we have no requirements or expectations -- whatever your beach wedding best outfit may be, works for us! Just a warning that the dinner restaurants have a formal dress requirement -- all that I know for sure is that there are no sleeveless t-shirts allowed (and probably no jeans or sandals?) Keep that in mind ;)
